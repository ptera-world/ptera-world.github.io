## what is this?

the essay collection. these are the pieces that needed more room than a fragment or a project description. they explore how software shapes people, how design encodes intent, what happens in the gap between structure and meaning.

## how to read

pick one that catches your eye. they're arranged spatially — essays that share something are near each other. you can also zoom out to see the shape of the whole collection, or use `/` to search.
